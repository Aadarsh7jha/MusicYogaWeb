Courses online:
Vocal as a natural self-tuning

The human voice is a natural self-tuning instrument, constantly adjusting pitch, tone, and resonance through muscle coordination and auditory feedback. The brain processes sound in real-time, allowing singers to correct pitch and refine vocal quality instinctively. This self-regulating system, involving the vocal cords, breath control, and resonance chambers, helps maintain harmony and expression. Unlike fixed instruments, the voice adapts dynamically to emotion, environment, and physical state, making it a unique, ever-evolving tool for communication and music.

Indian Classical Vocal (Raag-therapy)

Indian Classical Vocal music, especially Raag Therapy, is a healing practice that uses specific raagas to influence emotions and well-being. Each raag is associated with a particular mood, time of day, and energy, helping to balance the mind and body. Arun Duve, a practitioner in this field, emphasizes how vibrations and frequencies in raagas can reduce stress, improve focus, and promote inner peace. This ancient method aligns with the body's natural rhythms, making it a powerful tool for emotional and mental healing through sound.

Music & Yoga Union

Music and Yoga together create a powerful union, enhancing mindfulness and inner harmony. While yoga connects breath and movement, music deepens emotional expression and relaxation. Their combination promotes healing, balance, and spiritual growth, making the practice more immersive and transformative for the mind, body, and soul.

Mantra science

Mantra science is the study of sound vibrations and their impact on the mind and body. Chanting mantras creates specific frequencies that influence energy, enhance focus, and promote healing. This ancient practice aligns thoughts with higher consciousness, bringing peace, clarity, and a deep connection to the universe.

Harmonium

A harmonium course teaches the basics of playing, including finger techniques, scales, chords, and raagas. It covers melody, rhythm, and breath control for singing. Ideal for beginners and advanced learners, the course helps in devotional, classical, and contemporary music, enhancing musical expression and confidence.
